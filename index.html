Learning through fun

Edutainment Robotics challenges for students of all ages
Our main goal is to give students of all ages a taste for science and technology through the use of Edutainment Robotics.

Sign up and sign in

Please sign up to start your session, it is free. You need to sign up when you use the tool for the first time.

Connect your robot

Follow the instructions to connect your Thymio II or Cozmo robot. If you do not have a robot, you can also reserve a virtual robot online.

Program your mission

You can now program and test your robot in the Blockly language.
